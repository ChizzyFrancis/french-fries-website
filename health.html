Health & Nutrition Facts

A large serving of fast-food fries contains approximately:

NutrientAmount
Calories510 kcal
Total Fat24g
Saturated Fat3.5g
Carbohydrates66g
Sodium420mg

High-temperature frying creates acrylamide — a potential carcinogen. Healthier options include air-frying or oven-baking.
